SocialApp Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2023-10-07

SocialApp is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

What information do we collect?

We collect the following information when you use SocialApp:

Account information: Your name, email address, and profile picture.
Contact information: Your phone number and address book.
Content information: The posts, comments, and messages you share on SocialApp.
Usage information: How you use SocialApp, such as the features you use and the pages you visit.
Device information: The type of device you use and its settings, such as your operating system and browser type.
Location information: Your device's location, if you have enabled location sharing.
How do we use your information?

We use your information to provide you with the best possible experience on SocialApp. This includes:

Personalizing your experience: We use your information to show you relevant content and recommendations.
Connecting you with others: We use your information to help you find and connect with friends and family.
Providing customer support: We use your information to help you troubleshoot problems and answer your questions.
Improving SocialApp: We use your information to improve our products and services.
Who do we share your information with?

We only share your information with third parties when necessary to provide you with the service you have requested or when we have your consent. For example, we may share your information with third-party service providers who help us with tasks such as hosting our website or sending emails. We may also share your information with third-party advertisers, but only if you have opted in to receive advertising.

Your choices

You have choices about how we use your information. You can:

Opt out of receiving advertising: You can opt out of receiving advertising by going to your account settings.
Disable location sharing: You can disable location sharing by going to your device's settings.
Delete your account: You can delete your account by going to your account settings.
Security

We take the security of your information very seriously. We use a variety of security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated privacy policy on our website.
